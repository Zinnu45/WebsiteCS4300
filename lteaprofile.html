    About me:

    Helping the community with my spare time is what I enjoy! I am a full time Firearms Examiner at the Houston Forensic Science Center and like to do volunteer work to give back to my community. I like to play tennis, exercise, travel, play games, and spend time with family and friends. I currently going back to school to get a Bachelors' of Science in Computer Science because I realized I enjoy working on the computer and programming.

    As a Packages Coordinator, my job is to stay current on CDC updates with items that people may need due to the hardship caused by COVID-19. I understand that COVID-19 has put many people in hard situations mentally, physically, and financially. We hope that we could help as many people as possible in this time of crisis. Our goals at this non-profit organization is to help lessen the burden the panadmic has placed on this world.
