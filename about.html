    About Us:

    A group of friends and associates that have found each other having a similar passion to help the world.

    Many do not have the time to give, and we understand that. So we have made an effort to set time aside to help every day, and your support directly contributes to this.

    Our Cause:

    Founded in May of 2020, we have been working to serve our communities is the best way possible. Here we colaborate with pharmacies, clinics, and other buisnesses to gather the resources and funds to help provide a path to beating Covid-19. We strive to see not only our communities become and stay the most healthy, but the world as well. We recently have been able to collaborate with the worldwide organization Help the World to provide funding. From here we have gotte to see people without the funds and elderly without the support find comfort in proper medicine and support
